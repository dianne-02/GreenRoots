Our Story

2020 – The Beginning

GreenRoots began as a student-led dream to revive the forests of Davao through small yet powerful actions.

2022 – Expanding Roots

We partnered with schools and LGUs, planting thousands of trees and inspiring the youth to protect our planet.

2025 – The Movement Today

Today, GreenRoots thrives nationwide — proof that small seeds can indeed grow into great forests.
