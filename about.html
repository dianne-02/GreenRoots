Growing Together for a Greener Tomorrow

At GreenRoots, we believe that every tree we plant is a promise of life — a legacy for future generations.

Our Mission & Core Values

🌳 Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly practices and long-term forest restoration to heal our planet naturally.

🤝 Collaboration

We unite volunteers, communities, and partners to plant hope and strengthen environmental stewardship.

💚 Integrity

We operate with transparency and accountability, ensuring every donation nurtures the Earth.

Our Story

2020 – The Beginning

GreenRoots started as a small local project initiated by students who dreamed of reviving forests in Davao.

2022 – Expanding Roots

We grew our partnerships with local governments and schools, planting over 5,000 trees in rural areas.

2025 – The Movement Today

Now, GreenRoots continues to inspire communities across the Philippines to restore balance with nature.
